Background Information

LOONA (also translated as Girl of the Month) was a South Korean girl group created by Jaden Jeong under the company BlockBerry Creative. LOONA is a unique group because each member was debuted with their own single album, taking place between October 2016 and June 2018. This timeline included the debuts of the group’s 3 subgroups, named LOONA 1/3, ODD EYE CIRCLE, and yyxy respectively. The entire group officially debuted in August of 2018 along with the release of their first album, [+ +]. From then to June of 2022, LOONA released 5 EPs as a full group and 5 albums as well as 2 EPs total in the subgroups.
